Meet Your Guide

Dr. Elena Thorne, PhD

"Healing is not a linear path, but an unfolding of one's truest self in a space of radical safety."

With over fifteen years of clinical experience, my work is centered on the belief that every individual possesses an innate capacity for resilience. My role is to provide the architectural support for that resilience to flourish.

I specialize in navigating complex life transitions, anxiety, and the integration of trauma. My approach is deeply editorial—we look at the narrative of your life, keeping what serves you and gently rewriting the chapters that no longer do.

The Philosophy of Resilient Sanctuary

In a world that demands constant performance, therapy should be the one place where you are not a 'project' to be fixed. I treat our sessions as a sanctuary—a quiet, intentional space where we observe your internal landscape without judgment. We don't just solve problems; we cultivate a different way of being in the world.
